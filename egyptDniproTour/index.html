Beach vacation in Egypt

In fact, the bathing season in Egypt does not stop throughout the year, even in winter, Egyptian resorts continue to pamper tourists with warmth and sun. It is believed that the most favorable time for visiting this country is autumn. At this time in Egypt established not very hot, but comfortable weather for sunbathing and swimming.

Diving in Egypt

Excursions to the national park are organized for those who appreciate the beauty of the underwater world Ras Mohammed, which is known for its great variety of corals as well as other forms of marine life flora and fauna. The Hurghada region is also a hub for water sports enthusiasts such as: windsurfing, kiting,deep-sea fishing, swimming, all possible types of snorkeling and diving.

Excursions and entertainment in Egypt

Water parks are also the most popular excursions on the Sinai Peninsula. A quad safari, during which you can meet the dawn, or a trip to St. Catherine's Monastery and Mount Moses, where you can watch the incredibly beautiful sunset, is ideal for youth recreation. Regarding excursions for families with children, it is worth paying attention to various sea trips, the show "1001 Nights".
